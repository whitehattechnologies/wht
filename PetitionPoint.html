Petition Point Platform

Who has the petition (Tracking)

Let's face it, when you have hundreds, if not thousands of petitions printed.  Who the party responsible for that petition is needs to be clear.  Petition Point helps with that.

How many petitions (Tracking and Reporting)

No matter if it is a small or large amount, tracking the number of petitions that have been assigned to an individual or organization is key to getting them back completed.

Easy to Use (Process/Workflow)

Petion Point is designed with ease of use in mind.  The easier it is to gather and input, the more engaged the user is with the platform and the more accurate the data captured will be.  

Distribution Management (Tracking and Reporting)

PP offers bulk and single assignment of petitions for administrators. As well as a reassignment process for the field staff and volunteers. This provides the flexibility needed on the ground while capturing the organizational data needed to run an effective petition initiative.

Tracking/Reporting (Volunteer Management)

PP offers a secure dashboard for administrators that provides real-time updates being entered by volunteers on the field.  It also provide ongoing reports detailing performance analysis given anticipated metrics.

Petitions

The petition signature gathering process poses various obstacles for both experienced teams and volunteers.  White Hat Technology (WHT) has developed the Petition Point (tm) platform which allows for the assingment and ongoing tracking of petitions for both intiatives and candidates.
