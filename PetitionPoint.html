A modular approach

The petition signature process is difficult to manage. One of the key components is tracking the petitions that have been handed out to circulators and organizations in order to capture those for submission. Often in these initiatives, multiple petitions are lost due to not having tracked volunteer ownership of the petitions and helping to motivate circulators to complete and turn in their signed petition forms. White Hat Technologies (WHT) has developed a platform that allows for this form of tracking and offers you the ability to use Petiton Point Platform and may request custimzation.
